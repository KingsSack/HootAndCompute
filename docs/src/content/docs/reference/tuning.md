title: Tuning Your Robot
---

[Volt](../../api/Volt) can be used with [RoadRunner](https://rr.brott.dev/) or [PedroPathing](https://pedropathing.com/docs/pathing/installation). To use either of their pathing features, **tuning is required**.

## How to Tune [RoadRunner](https://rr.brott.dev/)

We recommend using the [RoadRunner Quickstart Guide](https://rr.brott.dev/docs/v1-0/tuning/) for tuning your robot.

## How to Tune [PedroPathing](https://pedropathing.com/docs/pathing/installation)

You can find a guide for tuning [PedroPathing](https://pedropathing.com/docs/pathing/installation) on the [PedroPathing Docs](https://pedropathing.com/docs/pathing/tuning).
